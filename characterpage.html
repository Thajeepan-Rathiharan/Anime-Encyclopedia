Naruto Shippuden is the second series of the Naruto anime that follows the main character Naruto Uzumaki on his quest to become Hokage. Unlike the first series, Naruto is now a few years older and has new friends and teachers to help him through his adventure. Later on in the series after the protagonists keep getting stronger gaining new powers, as do the antagonists who are making preperations for a war to take place that could end the ninja world.

Naruto Uzumaki

Naruto Uzumaki is the main character of the show appearing in episode 1 and is the best friend and rival of Sasuke Uchiha. He's part of Team 7 which is made up of him, Sasuke, Sakura and Kakashi, but Sasuke isn't part of it at the start of the series, so he goes on a quest to get his friend back in the first half of 'Shippuden'. Therefore he undergoes various training exercises with Kakashi and his friends to get stronger and gains new powers also learning to control the tailed beast sealed inside of him. At the time of war near the end of the series, he befriends the tailed beast inside him and gains even more strength and helps Naruto and his friends to hold against the new enemies they face. Hagoromo tells Naruto that he's the reincarnation of his youngest son Ashura Otsuski and explains his new powers he gives him and how he and Sasuke can defeat Kaguya. In the end him and Sasuke become best friends again and bring peacae back to the world.

Sasuke Uchiha

Sasuke Uchiha is introduced in episode 1 in his first appearance as the last Uchiha clan member as he was the only one spared by his brother Itachi Uchiha, who was the reason for the massacre of his clan. This leads him to wanting to get revenge and befriends Naruto, becoming best friends and then rivals at the end of the 'Naruto' series. In the 'Shippuden' series he's introduced again in episode 48 and him and Naruto are fighting in each interaction with each other. Later in the series in the was, Hagoromo explains that Sasuke and Naruto are the reincarnations of Madara and Hashirama and give them his sons Ashura and Indras powers to take down Kaguya and help end the war.

Hashirama Senju

Hashirama Senju is the First Hokage who was the rival of Madara Uchiha who were former best friends, but due to the tension between their clans and actions taken by his brother Tobirama Senju, it led to an all out war between the clans where Hashirama and Madara fought till one killed the other. In this case Hashirama won the clans and then after negotiations they were at peace and lived as one in the Hidden Leaf Village. His first appearance was in episode 70 in the 'Naruto' series, but is then bought back during the war to aid Naruto and the others by Orochimaru in the 'Shippuden' series in episode 370. Hashirama is told to be the reincarnation of Ashura Otsuski and rivals with Madara Uchiha in their generation.

Madara Uchiha

Madara Uchiha is one of the many villains in the show, but the most iconic one because of his entrance into the show in episode 322 when he enters the war. He was the one to save Obito when he was about to die and uses him as a puppet to bring him back to life via 'reanimation' in the war where they go through with their long awaited plan of taking over the world. Madara is told to be the reincarnation of Indra Otsuski in the generation after Ashura and Indras.

Hagoromo Otsuski

Orochimaru

Orochimaru was the main villain in the first half of the series after his first appearance in episode 27 and he is introduced as the teammate alongside Jiraiya and Tsunade being taught under the Third Hokage Hiruzen Sarutobi. Tsunade Senju

Tsunade Senju is the grandaughter of the First Hokage Hashirama Senju, due to this she was chosen to become the Fifth Hokage which she accepted after a speech from Naruto from her first appearance in 83. At the end of the first series, she takes Sakura as her student and trains her to become stronger and catch up with her teammates. Kakashi Hatake

His first appearance was in episode 3. Kakashi Hatake also goes by the name "Copy Ninja of 1000 jutsus", he is the leader of Team 7 which is formed of him, Naruto, Sasuke and Sakura. Obito Uchiha

Obito Uchiha is one of the main villains in the series who went by the names of Tobi and Madara up until the war where his mask was broken to reveal his identity. He was the classmate of Kakashi and Rin under the lead of Minato, he was in love with Rin but never got his chance to tell her as he witnessed Kakashi kill her from a distance when he was rushing to save her but was too late. Due to this he changed his dream of wanting to become Hokage and went down the path of revenge by plotting against everyone and teaming up with Madara Uchiha. The reason was because Madara was the one to save him when he was crushed by a giant boulder, but later on in the series he finds out Madara lied to him, to use him as his puppet and turns into a good guy aiding Naruto and the others in the war. His first appearance in the show was as Tobi is in episode 139, the episoode where he was revealed to be Obito was in episode 344.

Minato Namikaze

Minato Namikaze is the husband of Kushina Uzumaki and father of Naruto Uzumaki. He was known as the Fourth Hokage nicknamed the 'Yellow Flash of the Hidden Leaf' due to his teleportation technique and speed skills in combat. He was a former student of the Legendary Sannin Jiraiya the Gallant and progresed his way to the highest rank in the village becoming the Fourth Hokage. Sadly he died alongside his wife after saving his son and the Hidden Leaf Village from the rampage of the demonic Nine Tails rampage. His firt appearance in the series was in episode 168 where he talks to Naruto on a seperate occasion from his wife earlier in the series to stop his son from letting the Nine Tails take control over him. He made another appearance in the series when he was bought back to life through 'reanimaion' into the war at episode 372 alongside the previous 3 Hokages to put an end to the war.
